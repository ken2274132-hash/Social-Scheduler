## ğŸ”„ Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram
- **Image Size**: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1350 (portrait)
- **Video**: Up to 60 seconds for feed posts
- **Hashtags**: Add in caption, max 30

### Facebook
- **Image Size**: 1200x630 recommended
- **Videos**: MP4 format, up to 240 minutes
- **Best Time**: 1-4 PM on weekdays

### Pinterest
- **Pin Size**: 1000x1500 (2:3 ratio) recommended
- **Description**: Include keywords for search
- **Link**: Add product URL for traffic

### Shopify Products
- **Auto-sync**: Products update automatically
- **Inventory**: Low-stock items can be prioritized
- **Pricing**: Include price in captions for transparency
